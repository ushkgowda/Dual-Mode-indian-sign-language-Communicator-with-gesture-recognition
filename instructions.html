Instructions

    For gesture to speech conversion

  • Click on "Press to record video", the webcam window will open. Place your hand in the rectangle provided.
  • The alphabets will be captured. Click on "esc" key to stop the gesture capturing
  • You will listen voice of gesture and text
  • Click on "Translator" to convert text and voice to desired language
  • To get perfect output try to avoid background in the rectangle provided.

    • For speech to gesture conversion

  • Click on "Record" to record audio instantly and click on "Stop" to stop the recording. Click on "confirm"
  • Click on "Choose file" to upload audio from device.
  • Click on "Submit" to convert speech to text and gesture image
  • Speak clearly and try to avoid background noises.
