About


The Two-Way Indian Sign Language Translator initiative focused on facilitating real-time communication between sign language users and speakers of spoken language. includes converting gestures to speech, catering to situations where sign language users need to communicate with individuals who do not understand sign language. The ability to send emergency emails directly from the system ensures quick communication during urgent situations, which is crucial for accessibility and safety.
